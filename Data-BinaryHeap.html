data BinaryHeap (f :: Type -> Type) s a #

Binary tree backed by a vector.

0-based index

Left child is given as i .<<. 1 + 1. Right child is left child + 1. Parent is (i - 1) .>>. 1.

    0
  1   2
 3 4 5 6

Invariant

Parent value is smaller than or equal to their children.
