data StackSlidingFold s a #

Stack-based sliding window folding, going from left to right only. SWAG (sliding window aggregation).

    Fold of the elements in the buffer.

  • dualScanrSSF :: !(Queue s a)

    Right dual scanning, up to the last popped point. Works great with pop operations.
